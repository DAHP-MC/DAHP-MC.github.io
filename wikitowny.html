Rules & enforcement


Liable to change with minimal notice. If you're unsure, ask a towny Moderator or above to visit your build or show them online screenshots of examples.
  • Anti-AFK contraptions: Disallowed
  • Redstone farms: Disallowed
  • Auto fishing via right click: Disallowed
  • Auto smelt redstone contraptions: Allowed
  • Auto sort redstone contraptions: Allowed
  • Redstone farms which require player interaction, e.g. press a button and pistons get rid of sugar cane: Allowed
  • X-Ray (texturepack or mod): Disallowed.
  • Griefing & stealing: Fair game. This is allowed. Towny has protections built-in mayors can use. You can set a bounty on someone, or offer money for retrieval of your items via stealing it back. You can hire a mercenary or spy. Genuinely doesn't matter.
