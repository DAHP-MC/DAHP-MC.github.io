Auto Classes are announced in Chat every 2 hours and cycle through subjects each day. If you would like to see the timetable for auto classes, you can do .class in #house-elves channel on the Discord!



To enter the class, you can do /cj, /class join or click the message that appears in chat! When the class has started, you will have to stay seated and not talk in chat at all or you will be punished.



Each Class will have house point questions at the end of the class. To answer these, click the correct answer in chat. Do not try to answer in chat as you will get punished for disobeying a rule.
