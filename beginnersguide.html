Step by Step:
➟ First off, locate the rules quiz sign.
Before right clicking make sure to turn
your GUI scale into a smaller mode so you can successfully read all
of the questions. Then right click the sign and choose the correct answers.


➟ Now, you will be put in to a Wizard / Witch room. Neither of these will
affect your gameplay. You can go up to either side and click the button to
continue.



➟ After you click one of these options you will then be taken into
the next options room. You now have to pick your blood type. Again, this does
does not affect your gameplay. Just go up to a sign, right click it and then type
your name into the chat.

➟ Now, you will find yourself in a house. Go out of this house and follow the road.
Look for signs around you, they should be showing you the correct path towards the
Knightbus.

➟ Once you find the knightbus, right click Stan and start his quest. Then go up one floor
and stand in the middle of the bus. You should get teleported to the Leaky Cauldron.

➟ Now you should be outside of The Leaky Cauldron. Enter the Leaky Cauldron and go
straight to the back through the double doors. After that, right click on the Diagon
brick wall for it to open up. Run through this wall.

➟ Now, a Witch should be infront of you, right click this Witch and accept her quest.
After this, turn to your right and go into the Book shop. You will need to buy the first
row of books.

➟ Now travel to Ollivanders. The shop is at the back right of Diagon Alley.
When you arrive, speak to Ollivander and accept his quest. He is going to ask
you to go to the back of the shop, go to the tree and grab some wood and then
go into the mines to grab the wand core. Take these back to Ollivader and right click
him with the wand core and then the wand wood.

➟ Ollivander should have now given you a wand, left click it to test it out. If it fails please
click on the chat message that appears, this will give you another wand to test out.

➟ Travel back through Diagon Alley, go outside of the Leaky Cauldron and look for the glowstone.
Follow the glowstone to Kings Cross Station. Here you will need to find Platform Nine and Three Quaters.
Once you find the platform run straight at the wall.

➟ Next, you need to get on the train and sit down in the first compartment. After you sit down you should
arrive at Hogwarts. Speak to Hagrid and accept the quest. Then follow the path up to Hogwarts.



➟ You will find a boathouse, once there right click Hagrid, accept the quest and then sit down in the
boat. Here you will be teleported to the boathouse. Simply get up, speak to Professor Mcgonagall and
accept the quest. Now you can walk up to the Hogwarts Castle, go to the back of the hall and get Sorted.
Best of Luck!
